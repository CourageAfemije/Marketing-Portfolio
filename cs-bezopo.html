  • Managed Meta and Google Shopping campaigns driving 70% of total revenue

  • Implemented structured data and on-page SEO improvements, lifting organic traffic 55% in 4 months
  • Optimised product pages and checkout — improved conversion rate from 1.4% to 3.1%
  • Introduced a subscription upsell flow that increased average LTV by 34%
  • Achieved 5.2x ROAS on Google Shopping at peak (Q4 campaign)
