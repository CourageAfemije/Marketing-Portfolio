Key Responsibilities & Achievements

  • Developed the brand positioning, tone of voice, and visual identity guidelines
  • Launched paid campaigns across Meta and Google targeting diaspora communities in the UK and US
  • Built content marketing and SEO strategy that generated 4,000+ monthly organic visits within 5 months
  • Set up email nurture sequences that converted trial users into paying customers at 28%
  • Managed influencer partnerships with diaspora creators on YouTube and TikTok
  • Reduced cost per install from $18 to $6.40 through creative and audience testing
