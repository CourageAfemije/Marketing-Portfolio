Coca-Cola: Refresh the World

A full-funnel digital campaign that repositioned Coca-Cola's brand voice for Gen Z audiences across 8 markets — driving engagement, community, and measurable growth.

Client Coca-Cola
Industry FMCG / Beverage
Year 2024
Services Strategy · Digital · Creative

Overview

Challenge, Solution & Result

The Challenge

Coca-Cola's brand awareness among consumers aged 18–28 had declined significantly across digital channels. Engagement metrics were flat, content was perceived as outdated, and competitors were capturing the attention of the next generation.

My Solution

I developed a "Refresh the World" integrated campaign — combining short-form video, creator partnerships, in-feed activations, and an interactive microsite that placed community storytelling at the centre of every touchpoint.

The Result

Within 90 days, digital engagement increased by 312%. Brand sentiment among 18–28 year-olds rose 44 points. The campaign earned 2.1 billion impressions organically and was shortlisted for three industry awards.

Phase 01 — Discovery

Understanding the Audience First

I began with a 3-week immersion: audience interviews, social listening across TikTok, Instagram and YouTube, competitor benchmarking, and an audit of Coca-Cola's existing creative library. This gave me a clear picture of perception gaps and untapped cultural territory. Every creative decision that followed was anchored in this research.

Phase 02 — Strategy

Building a Framework That Scales

From the insights, I developed a content framework built on three creative territories: Shared Joy, Unfiltered Moments, and Local Pride. Each territory had its own tone, format mix, and creator archetype, allowing me to localise effectively across 8 markets without losing brand cohesion.

Phase 03 — Execution

Creative at Speed and Quality

I delivered over 200 unique creative assets in 6 weeks — including 34 short-form videos, 80+ still assets, and a fully custom microsite. I partnered with 28 micro-creators in each market, running a real-time content optimisation loop that shifted budget toward top-performing formats every 72 hours.
