Eyelevel

Head of Growth

1,100+ Active students (6 months)
$17 Cost per enrolment (from $45)

Eyelevel Learning is an online education platform offering professional skills courses. As Head of Growth, I owned the full acquisition and activation funnel — scaling enrolments while keeping cost per student acquisition within a profitable range.

Key Responsibilities & Achievements

  • Scaled enrolments from 200 to 1,100+ active students within 6 months
  • Designed a Meta Ads funnel: awareness (video) → consideration (free webinar) → conversion (course enrolment)
  • Reduced cost per enrolment from $45 to $17 through creative testing and funnel restructuring
  • Built an email onboarding sequence that reduced 30-day churn from 38% to 14%
  • Launched a student referral programme adding 180+ enrolments organically
  • Introduced YouTube pre-roll ads that generated 22% of top-of-funnel traffic at lowest CPM
